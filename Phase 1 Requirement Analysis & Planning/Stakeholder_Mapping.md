# Stakeholder Mapping

**Project:** Automated Employee Onboarding & Offboarding System
**Phase:** Requirement Analysis & Planning

---

## Description

HR, Managers, IT, Facilities, and Security teams collaborate through ServiceNow, each handling their specific onboarding and offboarding responsibilities. Role-based access ensures that stakeholders can only view and act on tasks relevant to their function.

---

## Key Stakeholders and Responsibilities

### 1. Human Resources (HR)

**Role**

* Initiates onboarding and offboarding requests.
* Maintains employee records and documentation.

**Responsibilities**

* Submit onboarding and offboarding requests in ServiceNow.
* Verify employee information and documentation.
* Coordinate with other departments during the employee lifecycle.

---

### 2. Managers

**Role**

* Approve onboarding and offboarding requests.
* Monitor employee lifecycle progress.

**Responsibilities**

* Approve or reject onboarding requests.
* Approve offboarding processes when an employee leaves.
* Ensure department-level tasks are completed.

---

### 3. IT Department

**Role**

* Provide technical access and resources to employees.

**Responsibilities**

* Create user accounts and system credentials.
* Provide access to required applications and systems.
* Disable system access during offboarding.

---

### 4. Facilities Team

**Role**

* Manage workplace resources for employees.

**Responsibilities**

* Allocate workspace, desk, or office resources.
* Provide required equipment or office supplies.
* Collect company assets during offboarding.

---

### 5. Security Team

**Role**

* Ensure organizational security and compliance.

**Responsibilities**

* Grant physical access to office premises.
* Monitor security policies and procedures.
* Revoke building access during offboarding.

---

## Expected Outcome

* Clear responsibility distribution among departments.
* Efficient collaboration between stakeholders.
* Secure and structured employee onboarding and offboarding process.
